Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Nexettel ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679).

Nexettel is a brand operated by Vendoprime e.U., a registered Austrian sole trader (Einzelunternehmen) based in Vienna, Austria.

Data We Collect

MVP Phase (current): No personal data is stored on our servers. Step progress, language preference, and onboarding status are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences only.

Firebase Authentication (if you create an account): We store your email address and display name via Google Firebase. Firebase is GDPR-compliant and stores data in EU data centres.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your progress across app restarts
  • To remember your language preference
  • To authenticate you securely (email/password or Google)
  • We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • We never use your data for advertising purposes

NexBot AI Chat

Your chat messages are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing. Messages are not stored permanently by Nexettel.

Please review Google's AI data usage policy at ai.google.dev. Do not share sensitive personal information (passport numbers, bank details) in chat messages.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Article 17)
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: Vendoprime19@gmail.com

Data Retention

Local device data: Retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Firebase account data: Retained until you delete your account via Profile > Settings > Delete Account. All cloud data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion, in compliance with GDPR Article 17.

Third-Party Services

Nexettel uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, optional cloud sync)
  • Google Gemini AI (NexBot chat responses)
  • Google Play (app distribution, in-app purchases)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We only share data with these services to the extent necessary to provide the app's functionality.

Children's Privacy

Nexettel is not intended for use by children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at Vendoprime19@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
